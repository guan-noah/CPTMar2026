How to Play 🎮

  • Words fall from the sky.
  • Type a word and press Enter or Space to destroy it.
  • You have 3 lives ❤️. Lose one when a word hits the ground.
  • Longer words give more XP! ⚡
  • Earn an additional 200 XP per level to level up — words get faster! 🚀
  • Red glow means a word is about to fall! ⚠️
  • ✨ Powerup bubbles appear during the game — type them for special effects:
  •   💛 Extra life — type words like life, heart, revive for +1 life (up to 5)
  •   🧹 Clear — type words like clear, purge, obliterate to wipe all words
  •   🐢 Slow time — type words like slow, freeze, stasis to slow falling words
  •   ⏳ Slow spawn — type words like reduce, limit, suppress to slow new words appearing
