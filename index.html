HOW TO PLAY

  • Words fall from the top of the screen.
  • Type a word and press ENTER to destroy it.
  • Type fast — words typed high up earn a distance bonus!
  • If a word reaches the bottom you lose a ❤️ life.
  • Lose all 3 lives and the game ends.
  • Earn enough XP to level up — new backgrounds unlock!
